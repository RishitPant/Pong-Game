For Player Vs A.I (Filename: Pong_1player.py)

1) Use arrow keys to move your player (Up and Down)
2) Sound is included so you can play with speaker on

For Player Vs Player (Filename: Pong2_player.py)

1) Green Player can move with arrow keys (Up and Down)
2) Red Player can move with keys: w, s (Up and Down)

HOW TO RUN:
Open command prompt at your game file location
Then type filename.py and hit enter to run your game!
